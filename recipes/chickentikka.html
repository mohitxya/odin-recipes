Chicken Tikka Masala

Simple recipe to cook "Chicken Tikka Masala".

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, garlic, ginger, and salt to create the marinade. Add chicken pieces to the marinade, coat well, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and place them on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Alternatively, grill the chicken for a smoky flavor.

  3. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in tomato puree and cook for another 5 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly.

  4. Add paprika, garam masala, and salt to the sauce, stirring to combine. Slowly stir in the heavy cream, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Add the baked or grilled chicken pieces to the sauce, ensuring they are fully coated. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.

  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or basmati rice.
